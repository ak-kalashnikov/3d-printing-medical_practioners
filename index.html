Course Overview

This course provides an in-depth understanding of 3D printing technologies for medical applications — from materials and design principles to regulatory and clinical considerations.

Modules

  • Module 1: Introduction to 3D Printing Technologies
  • Module 2: Biocompatible Materials and Design Constraints
  • Module 3: CAD Modeling and Digital Workflow
  • Module 4: Quality Control and Validation
  • Module 5: Regulatory and Ethical Frameworks
